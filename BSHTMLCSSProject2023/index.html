Enterprise/Propritary software

Enterprise software, also known as proprietary software, is developed and owned by specific entities, restricting its access and modification. Unlike open-source software, it's not freely accessible to the public. These applications are tailored for businesses, offering unique functionalities and services, but they typically come with licenses and subscription models for usage.
