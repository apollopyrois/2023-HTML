Adventure Glossary

ATV
All-Terrain Vehicle, a rugged vehicle designed for off-road adventures.
Belaying
The technique of controlling a rope during climbing to provide safety to the climber.
Geocaching
An outdoor recreational activity where participants use GPS to hide and seek containers, called "geocaches" or "caches," at specific locations marked by coordinates.
Zipline
A pulley suspended on a cable, typically made of stainless steel, mounted on a slope. It is designed to enable a user propelled by gravity to travel from the top to the bottom of the inclined cable by holding on to or attaching to the freely moving pulley.
Base Jumping
An extreme sport involving jumping from a fixed object, with a parachute to slow the fall before landing.
Via Ferrata
An Italian term which means "iron way." It is a protected climbing route found in the Alps and other locations. The term "via ferrata" is used in most countries and languages except notably in German-speaking countries which use Klettersteig, "climbing path".
